How it works

Your district runs campaigns. SchoolBridge handles the rest.

Set up a campaign in minutes. The AI agent takes it from there — reaching families, following up, and reporting results back to you.

1

Upload your family list

Sync from your SIS or upload a CSV. SchoolBridge validates contacts, detects language preferences, and identifies the best channel per family.

2

Set your campaign goal

Attendance outreach? IEP scheduling? Title I enrollment? Choose a template or write your own. The AI personalizes every message automatically.

3

The agent goes to work

Messages go out via SMS, email, or voice. Non-responders get intelligent follow-ups. Replies are handled by the AI — escalating to staff only when needed.

4

Review outcomes

Your dashboard shows engagement rates, conversations, and open items in real time. Export compliance reports for Title I documentation.

5

Families get heard

Two-way conversations mean families can ask questions, book meetings, and get resources — all without picking up the phone and waiting on hold.

6

You save time and money

Most districts recoup the annual subscription cost within the first campaign by eliminating manual follow-up and staff phone-banking time.
